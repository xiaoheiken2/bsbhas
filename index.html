Mold growth is a serious problem in USA due to the region's high humidity levels and common storm-related water intrusions from tropical storms and heavy rain. Mold needs moisture to grow. Scientific Mold Testing & Analysis


We collect and analyze air and surface samples to pinpoint mold species in your property. This helps tailor our cleanup strategy and ensures we utilize appropriate techniques for each mold species.

After the on-site evaluation, if mold is suspected but not confirmed, FreshStart performs scientific mold testing to clarify the extent and nature of contamination.

Moisture Control and Prevention


Addressing the underlying moisture sources is crucial to preventing future mold growth. We locate and correct sources like structural leaks and poor ventilation to ensure mold has no place to thrive.

Since mold thrives in damp environments, FreshStart prioritizes ongoing moisture management. After remediation, we assess all moisture-prone areas and apply corrective measures.

These may include:

  • - Fixing leaks in plumbing, roofing, or foundations
  • - Using commercial-grade dehumidifiers where necessary
  • - Enhancing air circulation in enclosed spaces
  • - Waterproofing solutions like sump pumps, vapor barriers, and French drains
  • - Helping homeowners understand how to keep indoor humidity in check
